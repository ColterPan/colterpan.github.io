LinkedIn Job Auto-Finder Bot

📸 Add screenshot

Upload images/Selenium1.png

Overview

Built a fully automated LinkedIn job search bot using Python and Selenium WebDriver. The bot logs into LinkedIn, searches across 19 targeted job queries in Singapore, scrapes and keyword-filters job listings, and exports all matched results to a structured CSV file — eliminating hours of manual job hunting.

Key Features

  • Automated LinkedIn login and multi-query job search across 19 role categories
  • Smart keyword filtering across 60+ role-relevant terms (audit, data, automation, BI, SQL, Python, etc.)
  • Deduplication engine using job IDs to prevent duplicate entries across queries
  • Scroll-based dynamic content loading to capture all paginated job cards
  • Extracts job title, company, location, and direct LinkedIn job link per listing
  • Exports all matched jobs to jobs.csv for easy review and tracking
  • Covers up to 10 pages per query — up to 250 listings per search term
