NutriBot – AI Health & Nutrition App

📸 Add your project screenshot here

Upload images/Hackcathon1.png

Overview

NutriBot is a full-stack AI-powered health and nutrition web application built for the UOL Computer Science Hackathon 2025 at Singapore Institute of Management (SIM). The app helps users bust common health myths, plan personalised weight loss or gain programmes, track their weight journey, and get healthier through AI-driven nutrition advice and smart pantry management.

Key Features

  • AI Nutrition Chatbot — GPT-3.5 powered chatbot that answers personalised nutrition, diet, and health questions based on the user's profile (age, weight, height, gender, BMI)
  • Health Myth Busting — Users ask about common health myths and receive evidence-based, personalised responses from the AI
  • Weight Loss / Gain Programme — Conversational goal-setting flow where users set a target weight; the app tracks weekly progress, calculates BMI, and awards milestones (First Log, Weekly Warrior, Goal Achieved, 5kg Change)
  • AI Food Image Detection — Users upload a photo of food; GPT-4o Vision automatically detects ingredients and adds them to the user's pantry
  • Smart Recipe Generator — Generates personalised recipes from pantry ingredients with full nutritional breakdown (calories, protein, carbs, fat)
  • Weight Journey Dashboard — Visual weight tracking with chart, progress indicator, milestone badges, and goal-aware colour coding
  • BMI & Calorie Calculator — Auto-calculates BMI category and daily calorie needs using the Mifflin-St Jeor formula on every profile update and weight log
  • User Auth & Profile System — Full signup, login, logout with JSON-based persistent storage and profile setup wizard

How It Solves Real Problems

  • Most people don't know which health advice to trust — NutriBot provides evidence-based, profile-aware answers instead of generic internet results
  • Weight tracking apps are often complex — NutriBot uses natural conversation to set and track goals without manual calculations
  • People waste food or don't know what to cook — AI image detection turns fridge photos into a smart pantry and generates healthy recipes instantly
